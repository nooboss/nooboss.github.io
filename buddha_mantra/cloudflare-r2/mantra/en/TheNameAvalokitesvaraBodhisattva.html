The Name of Avalokitesvara Bodhisattva

Namo Avalokitesvara Bodhisattva

Homage to the Great Compassionate Avalokitesvara Bodhisattva

1. Meaning

Avalokitesvara Bodhisattva — also known as the Bodhisattva of Great Compassion — embodies the profound ability to listen to the cries of all beings and respond with boundless kindness.

Reciting His holy name nurtures compassion, calms fear, and brings peace to the heart. It is a central practice in Mahayana Buddhism for invoking protection, guidance, and emotional healing.

Each variation of the recitation deepens the connection to His compassionate presence, helping practitioners cultivate wisdom, empathy, and serenity.

2. Benefits of Recitation

  • Reduces fear, anxiety, and emotional suffering.
  • Opens the heart to compassion, kindness, and understanding.
  • Provides spiritual protection and support in difficult times.
  • Brings mental calmness, clarity, and emotional balance.
  • Strengthens the connection to Avalokitesvara’s compassionate guidance.
