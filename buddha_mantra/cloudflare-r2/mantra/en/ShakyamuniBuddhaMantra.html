Shakyamuni Buddha Mantra

Om Muni Muni Mahamuni Sakyamuni Soha

ॐ मुनि मुनि महामुनि शाक्यमुनि स्वाहा

1. Meaning

This is the praise mantra of Shakyamuni Buddha — the Fully Awakened One who discovered the path to liberation for all beings.

Reciting this mantra helps cultivate wisdom, dispel ignorance, strengthen mindfulness, and develop compassion in alignment with the Buddha’s teachings.

The mantra awakens the inherent potential for enlightenment within us, guiding us back to clarity, peace, and inner freedom.

2. Explanation of Each Phrase

- OM
A sacred syllable that purifies body, speech, and mind.

- MUNI
Means “sage,” “wise one,” or “the awakened one.” Repeated twice to emphasize the Buddha’s profound wisdom.

- MAHAMUNI
“Great Sage” — honoring the supreme enlightenment of the Buddha.

- SAKYAMUNI
“The Sage of the Shakya Clan” — the full honorific name of Shakyamuni Buddha.

- SOHA
“May this be accomplished,” a seal of completion and aspiration.

3. Benefits of Recitation

  • Enhances mindfulness, clarity, and awareness in everyday life.
  • Reduces anxiety, emotional turbulence, anger, and mental restlessness.
  • Cultivates compassion, kindness, and patience toward oneself and others.
  • Helps dispel ignorance and supports insight into the true nature of reality.
  • Generates positive energy and brings deep inner peace and stability.
