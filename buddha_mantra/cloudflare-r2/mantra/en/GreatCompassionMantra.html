1. Meaning

The Great Compassion Mantra (Mahākaruṇā Dhāraṇī) is a sacred dharani associated with Avalokitesvara Bodhisattva, the embodiment of boundless compassion in Mahayana Buddhism.

It is one of the most widely recited mantras and is believed to bring protection, purification, and deep inner peace to those who chant it with sincerity.

The essence of the mantra is to awaken great compassion — the ability to listen deeply, understand suffering, and respond with wisdom and love, just as Avalokitesvara does.

2. Benefits of Recitation

  • Strengthens compassion, patience, and emotional resilience.
  • Dissolves fear, anxiety, and mental distress.
  • Purifies negative karma and removes obstacles in life.
  • Brings protection, peace, and spiritual support.
  • Calms the mind and enhances focus, clarity, and insight.
