Ksiti Garbha Bodhisattva Mantra

Om Ha Ha Ha Win Sam Mo Ti Soha

ॐ ह ह ह विस्मये स्वाहा

1. Meaning

This is the sacred mantra of Kṣitigarbha Bodhisattva — the Great Vow Bodhisattva who rescues beings suffering in the lower realms, especially those trapped in darkness, fear, or heavy karmic conditions.

The mantra represents compassion, fearlessness, determination, and the power to transform deep-rooted negative karma.

Reciting it helps practitioners connect with Kṣitigarbha’s protective and liberating energy.

2. Explanation of Each Phrase

- OM
A sacred syllable that purifies body, speech, and mind.

- HA HA HA
Three sacred sounds symbolizing the transformation of the “three poisons”: greed, anger, and ignorance. They also represent breaking through fear, darkness, and obstacles.

- WIN SAM MO TI
Symbolizes Kṣitigarbha’s fearless wisdom and liberating power, guiding beings beyond suffering and heavy karmic burdens.

- SOHA
“May this be accomplished” — the seal of completion.

3. Benefits of Recitation

  • Helps purify heavy or deeply rooted karma.
  • Reduces fear, anxiety, sadness, and emotional burden.
  • Provides protection from negative energies and unseen obstacles.
  • Builds courage, stability, perseverance, and inner strength.
  • Supports prayers for departed loved ones and offers comfort to wandering spirits.
