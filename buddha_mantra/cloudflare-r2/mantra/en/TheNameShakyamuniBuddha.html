The Name of Shakyamuni Buddha

Namo Shakyamuni Buddha

Homage to our Original Teacher Shakyamuni Buddha

1. Meaning

The phrase “Namo Shakyamuni Buddha” is the recitation of the name of Shakyamuni Buddha — the historical Buddha, the Fully Enlightened One, and the Teacher of our world.

“Original Teacher” refers to the Buddha as the root teacher who compassionately revealed the path to liberation for all beings. Reciting His name expresses gratitude, reverence, and refuge in His awakening.

Chanting this sacred name brings the mind back to mindfulness, compassion, and the clarity taught by the Buddha, supporting wisdom in daily life.

2. Benefits of Recitation

  • Calms the mind and reduces stress, worry, and confusion.
  • Cultivates gratitude and deep respect for the teachings.
  • Strengthens mindfulness, awareness, and right understanding.
  • Encourages moral conduct and a wholesome way of living.
  • Brings inner peace, stability, and clarity of purpose.
