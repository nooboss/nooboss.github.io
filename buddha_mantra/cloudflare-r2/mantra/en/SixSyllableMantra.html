Six Syllable Mantra

Om Mani Padme Hum

ॐ मणि पद्मे हूँ

1. Meaning

This is the Six-Syllable Great Bright Mantra of Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva (Guanyin). It represents compassion and wisdom, helping purify body – speech – mind and nurture loving-kindness toward all beings.

It can be understood as: “May my mind become pure, cultivating compassion and wisdom, so that I may progress toward enlightenment like Avalokiteśvara Bodhisattva.”

A short interpretation: “The jewel in the lotus” — symbolizing the clarity and compassion inherent within every person.

2. Explanation of Each Syllable

- OM
Purifies body, speech, and mind.

- MANI
Means “jewel”; symbolizes compassion.

- PADME
Means “lotus”; symbolizes wisdom that overcomes ignorance.

- HUM
The unifying power of compassion (MANI) and wisdom (PADME), leading to enlightenment.

3. Benefits of Recitation

  • Increases compassion
  • Reduces anger and anxiety
  • Purifies karmic obstacles
  • Brings peace and mindfulness in the present moment
