The Name of Amitabha Buddha

Namo Amitabha Buddha

1. Meaning

The phrase “Namo Amitabha Buddha” is the recitation of the name of Amitabha — the Buddha of Infinite Light and Infinite Life in the Pure Land tradition.

His name embodies boundless compassion, profound wisdom, and liberating power. Reciting it purifies the mind, opens the heart, and strengthens faith on the path to awakening.

To chant the Buddha’s name is to take refuge, invite clarity and peace, and align oneself with the light of enlightenment.

2. Benefits of Recitation

  • Calms the mind and reduces anxiety and mental suffering.
  • Purifies negative karma and clears inner obscurations.
  • Cultivates compassion, faith, and deep inner peace.
  • Strengthens mindfulness, focus, and spiritual insight.
  • Supports rebirth in the Pure Land for those with sincere faith and aspiration.
