Manjushri Mantra

Om Ah Ra Pa Tsa Na Dhi

ॐ अ र प च न धी

1. Meaning

This is the sacred mantra of Manjushri — the Bodhisattva of supreme wisdom, clear insight, and perfect understanding of the true nature of all phenomena.

The mantra increases clarity, intelligence, memory, and understanding of the Dharma. It helps cut through ignorance and confusion.

Practitioners who recite this mantra gradually awaken sharp wisdom, courage, and the ability to see things as they truly are.

2. Explanation of Each Phrase

- OM
Purifies body, speech, and mind, preparing for wisdom.

- AH
Represents openness, non-attachment, and the beginning of insight.

- RA
Discriminating wisdom that cuts through wrong views.

- PA
Deep understanding and strong memory.

- TSA
Penetrating wisdom that sees ultimate truth.

- NA
Clear reasoning and sharp analytical thinking.

- DHI
The seed syllable of wisdom — the sound that awakens transcendental knowledge.

3. Benefits of Recitation

  • Awakens profound wisdom and clearer understanding of the Dharma.
  • Improves memory, concentration, and learning ability.
  • Cuts through ignorance, confusion, and wrong perceptions.
  • Brings mental clarity, awareness, and inner confidence.
  • Cultivates courage, insight, and wise decision-making.
