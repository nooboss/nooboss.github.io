Red Tara Buddha Mantra

Om Kurukule Hrih Soha

ॐ कुरुकुल्ले ह्री स्वाहा

1. Meaning

This is the mantra of Red Tara Buddha (Kurukullā) — the fierce yet compassionate manifestation of enlightened magnetizing energy.

The mantra symbolizes the transformative power of love, the ability to attract positive conditions, and the capacity to influence situations through wisdom and compassion.

Red Tara Buddha helps practitioners cultivate loving magnetism, confidence, and the ability to harmonize relationships.

2. Explanation of Each Phrase

- OM
Purifies body, speech, and mind.

- KURUKULE
The name of Red Tara Buddha — the Buddha of magnetizing, attraction, and compassionate influence.

- HRIH
The seed syllable of powerful compassion and awakened wisdom.

- SOHA
“May this be accomplished,” sealing the blessing.

3. Benefits of Recitation

  • Attracts positive conditions, opportunities, and supportive connections.
  • Transforms anger, conflict, and emotional tensions.
  • Strengthens confidence, loving presence, and inner power.
  • Improves relationships through gentle influence and compassion.
  • Provides protection from harmful intentions and negative energies.
