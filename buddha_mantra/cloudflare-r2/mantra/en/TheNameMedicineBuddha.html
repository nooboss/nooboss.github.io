The Name of the Medicine Buddha

Namo Medicine Buddha, the Master of Lapis Lazuli Light

Homage to the Medicine Buddha, King of Lapis Lazuli Light

1. Meaning

The name “Medicine Buddha, King of Lapis Lazuli Light” is the reverent invocation of the Buddha of healing, purification, and radiant blue light.

His name represents the power to cure illness, purify negative karma, remove suffering, and restore harmony to both body and mind.

Reciting the Medicine Buddha’s name deepens faith, awakens compassion, and connects practitioners to His healing and protective blessings.

2. Benefits of Recitation

  • Brings healing to physical and mental illness.
  • Purifies negative karma and the root causes of suffering.
  • Increases vitality, longevity, peace, and well-being.
  • Cultivates compassion, clarity, and wholesome intention.
  • Offers protection and strength in times of fear or difficulty.
