Amitabha Buddha Mantra

Om Ami Dewa Hri

ॐ अमिदेव ह्री

1. Meaning

This is the mantra of Amitabha Buddha — the Buddha of Infinite Light and Infinite Life. Reciting this mantra connects practitioners with His boundless compassion, radiance, and protection.

The mantra invokes Amitabha’s purifying light, which dispels negative karma, calms the mind, and inspires deep faith in the Pure Land path.

It leads the practitioner toward peace, fearlessness, and the awakening of the pure, luminous nature within.

2. Explanation of Each Phrase

- OM
A sacred seed syllable that purifies body, speech, and mind.

- AMI DEWA
“Amitabha Buddha” — the Buddha of Infinite Light; “Dewa” means “the Radiant One.”

- HRI
The bīja (seed syllable) of Amitabha, symbolizing infinite compassion and liberating wisdom.

3. Benefits of Recitation

  • Purifies negative karma and alleviates emotional suffering.
  • Generates compassion, calmness, and deep inner peace.
  • Reduces fear, anxiety, and mental restlessness.
  • Connects the practitioner with Amitabha’s protective and illuminating presence.
  • Supports rebirth in the Pure Land for those with sincere faith and aspiration.
