Green Tara Buddha Mantra

Om Tare Tuttare Ture Soha

ॐ तारे तुत्तारे तुरे स्वाहा

1. Meaning

This is the sacred mantra of Green Tara Buddha — the swift, compassionate goddess who protects, rescues, and liberates beings from suffering and fear.

The mantra brings protection, removes obstacles, dispels negative energies, and offers comfort during difficult times.

Green Tara Buddha is known as the “Mother of Compassion,” always ready to respond to those who call upon her.

2. Explanation of Each Phrase

- OM
Purifies body, speech, and mind.

- TARE
Calls upon Tara — the One who liberates beings from suffering.

- TUTTARE
Removes the eight great fears and major obstacles in life.

- TURE
Brings swift liberation, quick wisdom, and courageous action.

- SOHA
“May this be accomplished,” sealing the blessing.

3. Benefits of Recitation

  • Reduces fear, anxiety, and negative emotions.
  • Provides strong protection from dangers and harmful influences.
  • Removes obstacles, karmic hindrances, and difficulties.
  • Enhances wisdom, peace, and compassion.
  • Attracts good fortune, clarity, and emotional strength.
