The Name of Ksitigarbha Bodhisattva

Namo Ksitigarbha Bodhisattva

Homage to Ksitigarbha Bodhisattva, the Earth Store King

1. Meaning

Ksitigarbha Bodhisattva is the Bodhisattva of great vows, boundless compassion, and unshakable determination. He vows to liberate all beings suffering in darkness, fear, or heavy karmic conditions.

His name represents courage, patience, deep compassion, and unwavering protection. Reciting His name brings comfort, reduces fear, and helps dissolve karmic burdens.

The variation “Great Vow” refers to His profound promise: not to attain Buddhahood until all beings trapped in suffering are freed.

2. Benefits of Recitation

  • Lightens heavy karma and emotional burdens.
  • Reduces fear, anxiety, and feelings of hopelessness.
  • Provides strong protection from negative energies and unseen obstacles.
  • Brings inner peace, stability, courage, and endurance.
  • Supports prayers for the deceased and guides them toward liberation.
