Cundi Buddha Mother Mantra

Om Cale Cule Cundi Soha

ॐ चले चुले चुन्दे स्वाहा

1. Meaning

This is the sacred mantra of Cundi (Cundī), the Buddha Mother of Purity and the deity of the Eighteen Arms in Vajrayana Buddhism. She embodies the power to purify karma, remove obstacles, and grant wisdom.

The mantra carries the vibration of compassion, purity, and divine protection, helping practitioners clear hindrances and open the path to spiritual progress.

Cundi is regarded as the manifestation of liberating wisdom, guiding all beings beyond suffering toward clarity and inner peace.

2. Explanation of Each Phrase

- OM
A sacred syllable that purifies body, speech, and mind.

- CALE
Purifies visible obstacles and external hindrances.

- CULE
Purifies subtle karmic obstacles deep within the mind.

- CUNDI
The name of the Buddha Mother Cundi — the powerful and compassionate goddess of purity and liberation.

- SOHA
“May this be accomplished,” sealing the blessing.

3. Benefits of Recitation

  • Removes obstacles in life and on the spiritual path.
  • Purifies negative karma and deep-rooted afflictions.
  • Enhances wisdom, clarity, and determination.
  • Cultivates compassion, patience, and mental peace.
  • Brings protection, good fortune, and spiritual support.
