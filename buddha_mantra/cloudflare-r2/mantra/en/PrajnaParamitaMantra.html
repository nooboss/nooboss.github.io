Prajna Paramita Mantra

Gate Gate Paragate Parasamgate Bodhi Soha

गते गते पारगते पारसंगते बोधि स्वाहा

1. Meaning

This is the concluding mantra of the Heart Sutra — the essence of Mahayana wisdom. It expresses the progressive journey of transcending illusion and reaching perfect enlightenment.

The whole mantra serves as a powerful call to advance beyond ignorance, step by step, toward the awakened state.

Short meaning: “Go — go — go beyond — go completely beyond — attain enlightenment!”

2. Explanation of Each Word

- GATE
“Go” — taking the first step beyond delusion.

- GATE
“Go further” — continuing the path with diligence.

- PARAGATE
“Go beyond to the other shore” — realizing nondual wisdom.

- PARASAMGATE
“Go completely beyond” — full penetration of ultimate truth.

- BODHI
Enlightenment — perfect awakening.

- SOHA
“May it be accomplished,” sealing the truth of the mantra.

3. Benefits of Recitation

  • Awakens deep Prajna wisdom beyond dualistic thinking.
  • Dissolves ignorance, confusion, and wrong perceptions.
  • Brings clarity, calmness, and insight in difficult moments.
  • Supports profound mindfulness and understanding of reality.
  • Guides practitioners steadily toward liberation.
