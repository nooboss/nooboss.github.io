Yellow Zambala Mantra

Om Dzambala Dzalendaye Soha

ॐ जम्भल जलन्द्राये स्वाहा

1. Meaning

This is the mantra of Yellow Zambala, the Buddhist deity of wealth, prosperity, generosity, and good fortune in the Tibetan tradition.

The mantra does not merely invoke material wealth — it cultivates the inner qualities of generosity, wisdom, and merit, which are the true sources of abundance.

Reciting the mantra helps practitioners open the flow of blessings, overcome poverty-related karma, and develop a compassionate and giving heart.

2. Explanation of Each Phrase

- OM
A sacred seed syllable that purifies body, speech, and mind.

- DZAMBALA
The name of Yellow Zambala — the deity representing prosperity, good fortune, and abundance.

- DZALENDAYE
“The One Who Grants Wealth and Protection” — symbolizing the wisdom that helps overcome financial hardship and obstacles.

- SOHA
“May this be accomplished,” sealing the blessing.

3. Benefits of Recitation

  • Attracts prosperous conditions and opens the flow of blessings.
  • Helps overcome financial obstacles and hardship.
  • Purifies karmic causes of poverty and scarcity.
  • Develops generosity, kindness, and a non-clinging mind.
  • Brings good fortune, stability, and inner confidence.
